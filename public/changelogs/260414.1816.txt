# <b>KanadeDX 260414.1816</b>

 <b>Feature</b>
• Android NFC Support: You can now swipe physical cards using NFC on Android devices.
• External Reader Support: For Android tablets without NFC, USB-connected card readers are now supported (HINATA only).
• Supported Cards: Scanning is restricted to official Amusement IC cards from the four major manufacturers and legacy Aime or Banapass compatible cards. Unofficial or non-standard cards are not supported.

 <b>Fix</b>
• Slider Precision: Sliders with decimals will no longer display precision up to the thousandths place.
